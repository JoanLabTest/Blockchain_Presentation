Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try the Professional version before committing?
Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan. You won't be charged until the end of this period, and you can cancel anytime from your client portal.
How much can I save with the annual subscription?
The annual plan is billed in a single payment and allows you to save approximately 20% compared to monthly billing. For the Pro plan, this reflects a price of €39/month (billed annually at €468) instead of €49/month.
How does Enterprise custom pricing work?
The Enterprise plan (starting at ~$50k ACV) is calculated based on your institution's size, active modules, and required API integrations. Our sales team will provide a precise quote after an initial qualification call.
Is my strategic data (simulations, portfolios) secure?
Absolutely. Institutional security is at the core of DCM. On the Enterprise plan, you have dedicated environments, DORA-compliant audit trails, and even air-gapped or on-premise deployment options if required.
