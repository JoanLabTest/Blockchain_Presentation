TFIN ID Standardization

Defining the unified identifier for Distributed Financial Instruments (ISO alignment roadmap).

The TFIN Standard (v1.0)

TFIN (Tokenized Financial Identifier) is a hierarchical naming convention designed to ensure interoperability across heterogeneous distributed ledgers (Ethereum, Canton, Polygon, Stellar).

TFIN - [ASSET_TYPE] - [INFRA] - [YEAR] - [SERIAL] Example: TFIN-BOND-POL-2025-0012

Identified as a critical requirement for MiCA-compliant cross-border settlement.

Roadmap to Institutional Adoption

Q1 2026
GitHub Open Specification

Q2 2026

Initial submission to ISO TC-307 (Blockchain and DLT) for formal review and alignment with ISO 20022.

Q4 2026
Global Standard Ratification

Finalization of the TFIN ID as a cross-chain identifier for institutional digital assets.
