Institutional Timeline & Market Progress

2020

Foundation: DLT Pilot Regime & wCBDC Early Research

Launch of consultations on the EU DLT Pilot Regime and intensive BIS research on Central Bank Digital Currencies.

2022

Acceleration: First Digital Bonds

Historical issuances by multilateral banks (EIB) and commercial banks (SocGen Forge) on public and private blockchains.

2024

Institutionalization: ECB Trials & Project Guardian

Start of ECB wholesale CBDC trials and global expansion of Project Guardian with major asset managers.

2025+

Maturity: Unified Ledger & Tokenized Deposits

Convergence toward the BIS Unified Ledger and the widespread use of tokenized commercial bank money.

Global Institutions

  • Bank for International Settlements (BIS) Central bank coordination, "Unified Ledger" research, and Innovation Hubs (BIS Innovation Hub).
  • IMF (International Monetary Fund) Macro-financial standards, global financial stability analysis, and cross-border payments.
  • World Bank Digital bond issuance for dev (e.g., Bond-i) and tokenized ESG standards.

Central Banks

  • European Central Bank (ECB) Digital Euro project, wholesale CBDC experiments, and TARGET Services settlement infrastructure.
  • Banque de France Leader in interbank DLT settlement tests and monetary integration of financial assets.
  • MAS (Singapore) Project Guardian: institutionalizing DeFi and tokenization of investment funds.

Market Infras (FMI)

  • Euroclear Deployment of D-FMI (Digital Financial Market Infrastructure) for digital securities settlement.
  • Clearstream / Deutsche Börse D7 platform for issuance of dematerialized and digital securities in compliance with eWpG.
  • DTCC Post-trade modernization via tokenization and integration of shared ledgers (DLT).

Regulators & Policy

  • ESMA (European Securities and Markets Authority) Supervision of the "DLT Pilot Regime" and regulatory convergence of crypto-assets (MiCA).
  • FSB (Financial Stability Board) Assessment of systemic risks related to tokenization and non-bank financial intermediation.
  • IOSCO International standards for investor protection on digital markets.
