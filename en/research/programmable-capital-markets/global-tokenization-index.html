The Four Pillars of GTI

Regulatory Implementation

Measures the presence of specific legal frameworks for DLT-based securities (e.g., Luxembourg Blockchain Act II, MiCA, UK Digital Securities Sandbox).

Issuance Velocity

Tracks the frequency and volume of natively issued tokenized instruments by regulated financial institutions and sovereign bodies.

Infrastructure Resilience

Evaluates the decentralization, security, and uptime of the DLT networks used for institutional capital markets issuance.

Interoperability Depth

Measures the capacity for cross-chain settlement and integration with existing RTGS systems (Real-Time Gross Settlement) or CBDCs.
