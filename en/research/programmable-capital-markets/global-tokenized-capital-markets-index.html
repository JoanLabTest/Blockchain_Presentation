The GTCMI is the primary institutional benchmark tracks the growth, depth, and liquidity of tokenized financial instruments worldwide. Mirroring the role of the MSCI World for equities, the GTCMI provides a single, weighted metric for the programmable finance era.

Index Methodology v1.0

Base Calculation

The GTCMI uses a market-capitalization-weighted formula. Every tokenized instrument compliant with TFIC levels 1-3 is included in the aggregate calculation: Index Level = Σ (MV / Base MV) × 100.

Temporal Base

The index is established with a Base 100 on January 1st, 2025. This anchor date serves as the inception point for tracking institutional adoption and market depth acceleration.

Eligibility Criteria

Included assets must be issued on institutional-grade DLT (public or private) and represent a direct legal claim on the underlying asset or its digital-native equivalent.

Data Sources

The index aggregates data from the Tokenized Capital Markets Observatory, primary DLT ledgers, and official disclosures from Tier-1 financial institutions.
