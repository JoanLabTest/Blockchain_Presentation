Executive Summary

DCM Digital's MRM architecture mathematically guarantees strict isolation between deterministic quantitative models and the natural language generation layer. This framework is designed to meet the strictest supervision requirements of financial regulators (ECB, BaFin, AMF).

Architecture Zero Hallucination

Separation of Concerns (SoC)

The system structurally isolates quantitative processing from qualitative formatting:

  • Quant Engine: Deterministic stress-test calculations.
  • Data Hook: Injection of verified variables into frozen prompts.
  • Narrative Engine: Translation of mathematical states into institutional formats (e.g., COMEX Brief).

Limitation des Triggers

The AI cannot be triggered by free text. It responds exclusively to algorithmic anomalies or action buttons pre-approved by the risk committee.
