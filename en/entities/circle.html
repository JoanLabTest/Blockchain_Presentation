Circle Internet Financial

The institutional standard for dollar stablecoins. Circle issues USDC, a fully-reserved digital dollar backed 1:1 by cash and US Treasuries held at systemically important financial institutions (SIFIs). Circle acts as the connective tissue linking public blockchains to TradFi capital markets.
