Institutional Advisory Board

The DCM Core Institute is supported by an independent board of experts ensuring the technical, legal, and economic integrity of our tokenization standards.

Legal & Regulatory

Technical Architecture

Market Infrastructure

FMI Integration

Economic Research

Market Intelligence

Validating quantitative methodologies for market capitalization monitoring and institutional adoption benchmarks.

Mission Statement

The Advisory Board serves as a primary review body for all technical standards (TFIC, TFIN) published by the Institute. Its mission is to provide independent validation and strategic direction to ensure that DCM Core assets remain at the forefront of the institutional programmable finance ecosystem.

By maintaining a multi-disciplinary board, the Institute balances technical innovation with rigorous legal compliance and economic accuracy, mirroring the governance models of international standard-setting bodies.
