About DCM Core Institute

The DCM Core Institute is a global reference authority dedicated to the standardization and observation of tokenized capital markets. By providing institutional-grade data (GTSR), technical frameworks (GTDS), and universal identification (TFIN ID), the Institute enables the transition toward programmable market infrastructures.
