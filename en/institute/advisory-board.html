Mandate & Mission

The Institutional Advisory Board (IAB) provides strategic oversight for the DCM Core Institute's research programs and standards hub. Composed of global leaders from central banking, top-tier financial institutions, and specialized legal practices, the IAB ensures that our frameworks remain both technically robust and practically implementable within existing global regulatory perimeters.
