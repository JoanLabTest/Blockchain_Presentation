Institutional FAQ

What is RWA Tokenization?

RWA tokenization involves representing traditional assets like bonds, real estate, or private equity as digital tokens on a DLT, drastically reducing operational friction.

How does DCM Core align with MiCA and DORA?

Our methodology integrates risk assessment frameworks that map directly to the reporting and oversight standards required for institutional digital asset service providers in the EU.
