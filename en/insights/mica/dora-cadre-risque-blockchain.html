DORA: Blockchain Resilience Framework for Institutions

Executive Summary: DORA redefines ICT resilience standards for DLT. This guide details redundancy, monitoring, and testing requirements to ensure the continuity of digital asset services.

The **Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)** regulation represents a paradigm shift for the European financial sector. For institutions deploying distributed ledger technologies (DLT), DORA is the ultimate test of operational reliability.

DORA Resilience FAQ

What is DORA in the context of blockchain?
The Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) establishes a harmonized ICT risk management framework for European financial institutions, including those using blockchain-based infrastructures.
How does DORA regulate third-party blockchain providers?
DORA mandates enhanced oversight and strict contractual obligations for critical ICT providers, including cloud providers or node infrastructure operators.
What is the relationship between DORA and MiCA?
MiCA focuses on market integrity and governance, while DORA ensures the operational resilience of digital infrastructures.
