Governance for Tokenized Assets (RWA)

Executive Summary: Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization is redefining the liquidity of illiquid assets. This guide explores the governance mechanisms necessary to securely and compliantly link physical assets to their digital twins.

**Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization** — such as real estate, bonds, or private equity funds — is the next growth engine for on-chain finance. However, tokenization is not just about creating a token; it requires a governance layer that links the physical asset to its digital representation.

RWA Tokenization FAQ

What is Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization?
It is the digitization of tangible assets (real estate, precious metals, infrastructure) or financial assets on a blockchain to facilitate their fractionalization and trading.
How to manage legal rights on-chain?
Through governance smart contracts that integrate business rules and legal constraints directly into the token's code (Security Tokens).
What is the advantage of fractionalization?
It lowers the entry threshold for large investments, thereby increasing the potential investor base and overall liquidity of the asset.
