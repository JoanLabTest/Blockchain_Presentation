COMEX Executive Brief: Governance OS

The Problem (Status Quo)

DLT initiatives (Tokens, CBDC) are IT-driven. Risk departments (L2) lack tools and frameworks to model the risk of these new infrastructures, leading to regulatory roadblocks or capital surcharges (Basel III).

The Solution (DCM Digital)

A sovereign OS that translates technical on-chain complexity into Board-ready metrics. DCM automates the SR 11-7 framework for AI/Quant models and generates cryptographic audit trails for DORA and MiCA.

Time-to-Value: ~30 Days (Pilot) ROI Target: OpRisk Capital Optimization

Three Pillars of Integrity

Transitioning from "black box" AI to a rigid and auditable intelligence layer.

Zero Hallucination

Structural separation between the calculation engine (Quant) and narrative generation. The AI never invents data; it contextualizes verified metrics.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every generated insight is timestamped and cryptographically linked to source data. Full traceability designed for Big Four audits.

Deterministic Triggers

The AI only acts on algorithmic triggers. If a stress variance crosses a defined threshold, the Copilot generates a supervision note.

Data Traceability

From raw ingestion (Network Nodes) to immutable storage. Every metric is attached to a verified audit trail.
