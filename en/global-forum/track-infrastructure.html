Infrastructure Track

DLT Pilot Regime & Beyond

Dedicated to building the settlement finality layer for tokenized sovereign bonds, this track convenes central securities depositories, clearing houses, and core banking technologists.

Key discussions include atomic settlement mechanisms, cross-chain atomic swaps under regulatory oversight, and the transition of experimental sandbox models to systemic, production-grade rails.
