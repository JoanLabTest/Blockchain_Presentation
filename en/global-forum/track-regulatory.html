Regulatory Track

Global Standards & Codification

Strategic Objectives

The Global Forum regulatory track focuses on the harmonization of digital finance frameworks. Our mission is to translate high-level principles into operational guidelines for Tier-1 institutions and systemic actors.

MiCA/DORA Compliance

Guidelines on asset segregation and digital operational resilience for systemic banks.

Cross-Border Interoperability

Mutual recognition standards for tokenized securities across major jurisdictions.
