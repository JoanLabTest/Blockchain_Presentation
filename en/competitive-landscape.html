Traditional GRC & MRM Tools

Representative Examples: IBM OpenPages, ServiceNow GRC

Designed to manage documentary compliance and closed statistical models (VaR, IRC). They intervene ex-post.

The gap filled by DCM: Dynamic integration of network states (Canton/SWIAT) into the regulatory framework in real-time.

Blockchain Analytics & Forensics

Representative Examples: Chainalysis, Elliptic, TRM Labs

Excellent for post-trade or crypto-native transactional investigation (AML/KYT). Totally disconnected from systemic capital requirements.

The gap filled by DCM: Translation of off-chain and on-chain data into prudential impact metrics for the CRO.

Gen-AI / LLM Wrappers

Representative Examples: BloombergGPT, Copilot for Finance

Provide powerful textual automation. Introduce the lethal "hallucination" risk when analyzing critical financial exposures.

The gap filled by DCM: AI under mathematical constraint. Cryptographic Audit Trail (Zero-Trust) proving the LLM never invents data.

Quant Risk Engines Classiques

Representative Examples: Murex, SAS Risk Management

Historical valuation engines. Blind to deterministic risks: a "third-party node IT failure" (pure OpRisk) is not priced algorithmically.

The gap filled by DCM: Systemic overlay integrating correlated DLT infrastructure failures and stablecoin depeg risks.
