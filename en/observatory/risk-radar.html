[TECH] Infrastructure
Evaluates smart contract maturity, protocol architecture, bug bounties, and time-in-market without exploit.
WEIGHT: 20%
[CUST] Counterparty
Assesses the reliability of the entity holding the underlying asset (Tier 1 Bank vs. Unregulated Trust) and oracle dependencies.
WEIGHT: 30%
[LEGAL] Regulatory
Measures alignment with clear jurisdictional frameworks (e.g., MiCA) versus operating in regulatory gray zones.
WEIGHT: 30%
[LIQ] Liquidity
Evaluates secondary market depth, ability to execute atomic T+0 fiat conversion, and redemption friction.
WEIGHT: 20%
