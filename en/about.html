About DCM Digital

Governance Intelligence Infrastructure

We build the governance infrastructure for financial institutions adopting distributed ledger technology.

Our Mission

Quantifying DLT Risk as Prudential Risk

DCM Digital provides risk and compliance departments with the measurement, simulation, and reporting tools necessary to integrate tokenized assets into existing regulatory frameworks.

Decision Intelligence

Multi-criteria scoring engine (Technical, Legal, Market, ESG) with statistical normalization to assess DLT infrastructure maturity.

Risk Governance

Native alignment with DORA, MiCA, and Basel III to transform blockchain metrics into actionable prudential indicators for regulators.

Data Integrity

Zero-Hallucination architecture with Data Pinning — every insight is cryptographically anchored to a Truth Layer of verified data.

Board Reporting

Generation of L2 certified reports with complete audit trails, from raw DLT flow to executive dashboards.
