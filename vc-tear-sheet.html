DCM DIGITAL

Enterprise DLT Governance & Capital Optimization OS (B2B SaaS)

Company Overview

DCM Digital provides the critical "Governance Layer" missing in enterprise blockchain deployments. By translating complex technical DLT events (Smart Contracts) into real-time, auditable financial risk metrics, we enable Tier 1 banks to comply with Basel III/DORA and drastically reduce their Operational Risk Capital Buffers, unlocking millions in liquidity.

The Problem & Market

The Gap: Traditional Model Risk Management (MRM) systems treat blockchain deployments as static "black boxes". This lack of deterministic observability forces regulators to mandate massive capital buffers (RWA) for Operational Risk.

TAM (Total Addressable Market): The Institutional Digital Asset software market is projected to reach $10B+ by 2030, driven by the tokenization of Real-World Assets (RWA) and mandatory frameworks like DORA in the EU.

The Product Solution (DCM Governance OS)

  • Continuous MRM Monitor: API-first Oracle bridging DLT environments (Canton, Hyperledger) with legacy Core Banking.
  • Capital Optimization Engine: Translates cryptographic proofs into LGD (Loss Given Default) reductions.
  • Data Trust Certification: Cryptographically verifiable audit trails for compliance teams (Line 2/3 Defense).

Competitive Moat

  • Regulatory Native: Built strictly for Basel III AMA / DORA compliance, not retail crypto.
  • High-Friction Enterprise Sticky: Deep integration into Core Banking Systems creates a +5 Year lifespan per client.
  • First-Mover in TradFi DLT Governance: Competitors focus on on-chain analytics (Chainalysis); we focus on balance-sheet impact.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Direct enterprise sales targeting Chief Risk Officers (CRO) and Heads of Digital Assets at European G-SIBs. High-touch, 6-9 month sales cycle prioritizing "Proof of Value" through parallel-run simulations demonstrating immediate capital relief.
