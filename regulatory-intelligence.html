Regulatory Timeline

Macro-evolution of global digital asset policies

MAY 2026 (Forward Outlook)

MiCA Technical Standards Finalization

European Union expected to finalize Level 2 and Level 3 technical standards for MiCA. Significant impact on stablecoin issuers (EMTs) and stringent requirements for major custody providers and institutional CASPs.

DEC 2024

MiCA Enforcement (Full CASP Rules)

Full application of the Markets in Crypto-Assets regulation across the EU for all Crypto-Asset Service Providers (exchanges, custodians, advisors), ending transitional national regimes.

JUN 2024

MiCA Enforcement (Stablecoins)

Rules regarding Asset-Referenced Tokens (ARTs) and E-Money Tokens (EMTs) take effect in the EU. Non-compliant offshore stablecoins begin facing delisting from major EU exchanges.

MAY 2023

MiCA Formal Adoption

The European Council formally adopts the MiCA regulation, establishing the world's first comprehensive legal framework for crypto-assets across 27 member states.

NOV 2022

FTX Collapse Catalyst

The collapse of FTX triggers a massive global regulatory acceleration. Focus shifts heavily toward custody segregation, proof of reserves, and strict oversight of centralized intermediaries.

SEP 2020

MiCA Proposal Initiated

The European Commission officially releases the first draft of the Markets in Crypto-Assets regulation as part of the Digital Finance Package.
