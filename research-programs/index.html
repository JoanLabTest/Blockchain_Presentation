Research Programs

As a prominent global think tank, our intellectual output is structured across five major strategic programs. We provide rigorous analysis for policymakers, market infrastructure providers, and institutional investors navigating the digital frontier.

Digital Asset Regulation & Compliance

Analysis of European and global regulatory frameworks (MiCA, MiFID II, AML/CFT) and their systemic compliance implications for service providers.

Lead: Marc-Antoine Lemarchand
Output: MiCA Compliance Guide

Systemic Risk in Crypto Markets

Pioneering the GDARI framework to monitor contagion, liquidity spirals, and macro-financial linkages between digital assets and traditional banking.

Lead: Dr. Alexandre Dubois
Output: Digital Asset Risk Index

Tokenization & Market Infrastructure

Evaluating DLT-based settlement layers, the Pilot Regime, and the operational viability of Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenization pipelines.

Lead: Elena Rostova
Output: Blockchain Bond Dashboard

Central Bank Digital Currency Architecture

Research mapping wholesale and retail CBDC initiatives, stablecoin reserve standards, and next-generation monetary interoperability.

Lead: Guest Network
Output: Digital Finance Observatory

Programmable Finance & DeFi Systems

Technical and economic assessments of smart contract resilience, algorithmic yield mechanics, and decentralized liquidity pools.

Lead: Guest Network
Output: Automated Yield Simulator
