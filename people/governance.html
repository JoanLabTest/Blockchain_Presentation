Editorial Governance & Ethics

The DCM Core Institute operates under strict academic and professional independence. All publications in the Working Papers series and the Policy Journal undergo rigorous double-blind peer review.

Peer-Review Standards

Our review process ensures technical accuracy, methodological soundness, and policy relevance. We follow the standards set by international financial bodies (BIS, ECB).

  • Initial Screening: Technical feasibility and alignment check by the Editor-in-Chief.
  • Double-Blind Review: Two independent external reviewers per paper.
  • Conflict of Interest: Strict disclosure mandates for all authors and fellows.

Editorial Board

The board oversees the strategic research direction and final publication approvals.
